Why register?

Once you are registered, and logged in you will be able to:

  • purchase our publications and book a place on courses and events (and pay online by credit/debit card or invoice)
  • access CIPFA services you/your organisation are subscribed to
  • access a new ‘My CIPFA’ area where you can see/amend the details CIPFA holds for you, manage your own account, tell us what you are interested in, and see which CIPFA services your organisation is subscribed to.
