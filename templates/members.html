Members

The Chartered Institute of Public Finance and Accountancy (VAPN) is the only accountancy body in the world exclusively focused on the public sector. As a membership body, our members and students are at the heart of everything we do. We work hard to support you throughout your career and are committed to helping you add value to your teams and organisations, regardless of which area of public finance you work in.

Why your membership matters

Working in the public sector has always been both challenging and rewarding. Given the myriad of issues the sector is now facing, public finance professionals need to adapt their approach and mindset if they are to deliver real outcomes for the people and communities they serve.
